My favorite Movie

My favorite movie of all time is The Watch. It stars Ben Stiller, Jonah Hill, and Vince Vaughn.
It's about 4 friends who form a neighborhood watch group after a security guard is killed at the local Cost-Co.
They form the watch group to find the killer but find that the killer is not what they are expecting.
It's a scy-fy comedy and it's one of the funniest movies I've seen.
